About In Tread

We would like to introduce you to out new bike-share service called InTread. InTread provides convenient and supplemental transportation options to students, staff and visitors alike on the Iowa State University campus.

Our mission is to provide the ability to travel independatnly without stress of time-consuming schedules. We would also like to provide convenient transportation alternatives for both leisure and expedited use at an affordable price.

This bike-share provides bikes with an alt terrain purpose. You will be able to conveniently travel around the Iowa State campus and Ames Area. This fee for the program included in your tuituion if you are an Iowa State student or staff member. If you are an Ames resident, you will be charged one dollar per half hour.


Proposed Functionality


Bike shelter

At each InTread location there will be a bike shelter. This shelter will serve as weather sheilding and as a holding cells for the bikes. The stand for the bike will have locking mechanism that will hold the bike in place until a user checks it out.

Paying Kiosk

A Kiosk will be located at each shelter, giving users a location to swipe their student id, or payment method, proir to taking the bike and again upon it's return.


Bike Information


Mountain Bike

The mountain bike comes equipt with a water bottle holder and bag/equipment holder. This bike is designed for all terrains, and can handle more intense riding styles

Town Bike

The town bike will have collapsible portfolio clips, as well as a bag/book container
This bike is for street and sidewalk riding
and will be efficient for travelling from point A to B
